Our Restaurant

Established in 1999, we are proud to be one of the first to present genuine, rustic, italian cuisine within glasgow. In the heart of Glasgow's Merchant City Esca & Chisholm Twenty Seven are the perfect place to meet with friends, work colleagues or business associates. We also cater for larger parties and events.

Our Ethos

Our fresh but forward thinking ethos blends modern and traditional Italian food within warm and comfortable surroundings. Coupled with an excellent selection of wine and prosecco it makes for a truly memorable dining experience.
